MISSION
OBJECTIVE

Chico State Formula SAE is a high-performance collegiate engineering organization dedicated to technical excellence and competitive racing. We provide a modular platform for students to gain hands-on experience in automotive design, simulation, and fabrication, bridging the gap between theoretical physics and real-world motorsport application.

Our mission is to engineer track ready racing machines; preparing the next generation of Chico State FSAE members with skills and oppotunities to excel in automotive engineering. Through SAE International competitions, we test the limits of our engineering DNA on the world’s most demanding circuits.

ENGINEERING &
DESIGN FOCUS

AERODYNAMICS

Utilizing advanced CFD to engineer high-downforce packages, including front/rear wings and underbody diffusers designed for maximum grip.

POWERTRAIN

Optimizing performance through custom fuel maps, intake plenum design, and specialized exhaust systems tuned for rapid acceleration and reliability.

ELECTRICAL

Designing integrated data acquisition, wiring harness architecture, and control systems to ensure reliable power distribution, real-time telemetry, and precise engine management.

SUSPENSION

Developing adjustable suspension geometry, optimized camber curves, and damper tuning strategies to enhance mechanical grip, stability, and driver confidence under dynamic load conditions.

DRIVETRAIN

Engineering efficient torque transfer through precision gearbox selection, differential tuning, and optimized final drive ratios to maximize acceleration and corner-exit performance.

CHASSIS

Designing chassis architecture and suspension kinematics. Focusing on tire data acquisition and damper tuning to ensure optimal contact patch performance.

NORCAL
SHOOTOUT

Competing at Thunderhill Raceway, the NorCal Shootout served as a high-intensity proving ground for dynamic performance and system reliability. Real-world track data validated aerodynamic balance, suspension tuning, and power delivery under sustained load conditions, strengthening our competitive foundation heading into national events.

Formula SAE Michigan represents the pinnacle of collegiate motorsport engineering.
